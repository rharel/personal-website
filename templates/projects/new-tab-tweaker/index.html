Recently, I came to the ironic realization that I encounter Firefox's bland-and-boring 'New Tab' page far more often that I do the wonderful wallpaper I carefully chose to decorate my Desktop with. So naturally, I headed over to Mozilla's add-on repository in search of an add-on that would allow me to decorate the New Tab page with a customized background image.

Alas, existing add-ons were either bloated with additional functionality that I didn't intend to use or based on legacy technology that will be phased out of Firefox in the very near future. So, I set out to develop my own add-on for the job, and took it as an opportunity to dive in and familiarize myself with up-and-coming WebExtension technology.
